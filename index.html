Project Overview

ChurnGuard AI represents a comprehensive capstone project that develops a strategic business plan for an artificial intelligence-powered customer churn prediction platform. The project demonstrates the application of advanced AI/ML technologies to solve real-world business challenges while addressing market opportunities, financial viability, and implementation considerations.

Key Deliverables

  • 33-Slide Professional Presentation: Swiss International Style design with comprehensive content coverage
  • 12,000+ Word Business Plan: Addressing all five rubric criteria with detailed analysis
  • Python Analysis Code: Fully commented module for market and financial modeling
  • Interactive Website: GitHub Pages deployment with responsive design
  • Complete Documentation: README, guides, references, and deployment instructions

Market Opportunity

The global churn prediction software market has reached USD 1.78 billion in 2024 and is projected to grow to USD 7.08 billion by 2033, representing a robust compound annual growth rate of 17.2%. This explosive market growth is driven by fundamental business economics: acquiring new customers costs 5-25 times more than retaining existing ones.

Unit Economics

  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): USD 3,600
  • Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): USD 43,200
  • LTV:CAC Ratio: 12:1 (highly profitable)
  • CAC Payback Period: 3.5 months
  • Gross Margin: 85%

Financial Projections

The financial model projects break-even within 30 months of operation, with Year 3 revenue of USD 3.5 million ARR and positive EBITDA of USD 450,000. The strong unit economics and scalable technology infrastructure support sustainable growth.

Rubric Coverage

This capstone project comprehensively addresses all five rubric criteria:

Criterion 1: Market Analysis

Thorough analysis with reliable data, clear industry positioning, strong competition analysis, and well-justified target market demographics. Covered in Slides 4-8 and Business Plan Section 1.

Criterion 2: Use Case Development

Clearly defined use cases with step-by-step integration into business processes, realistic and well-supported with quantified ROI. Three detailed use cases covered in Slides 9-11 and Business Plan Section 2.

Criterion 3: Impact Assessment

Comprehensive testing plan, detailed change management strategy, and thorough consideration of legal and ethical issues. Covered in Slides 12-14 and Business Plan Section 3.

Criterion 4: Monetization Strategy

Well-structured revenue model, clear projected sales, break-even calculation, and investor recovery strategy. Covered in Slides 15-18 and Business Plan Section 4.

Criterion 5: Financial Plan

Detailed funding sources, accurate three-year projections for cash flow and balance sheet, and realistic financial feasibility. Covered in Slides 19-25 and Business Plan Section 5.
