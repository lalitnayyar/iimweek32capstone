Success Metrics

Customer KPIs

We will monitor a comprehensive set of customer-focused KPIs to ensure product-market fit and sustainable growth. Customer churn rate is our north star metric, as our product's value proposition is directly tied to helping customers reduce their own churn. We target maintaining monthly churn below 5% through proactive customer success management.

Monthly Customer Churn Rate: Target <5% (north star metric)
Annual Customer Retention: Target >60% demonstrating product stickiness
Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Target >110% showing expansion revenue
Gross Revenue Retention: Target >90% indicating strong retention
Customer Health Score: 0-100 scale with 70% in green zone
NPS Score: Target >50 indicating strong customer satisfaction
