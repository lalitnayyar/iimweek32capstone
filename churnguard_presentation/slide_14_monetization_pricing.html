Pricing Model

ChurnGuard AI employs a value-based pricing strategy with three core tiers designed to serve businesses of all sizes. The Starter plan at $499/month targets SMEs with up to 10,000 customers, providing essential churn prediction capabilities and basic integrations. The Professional plan at $1,499/month serves mid-market companies with up to 50,000 customers, adding advanced analytics, API access, and multiple predictive models.

Tiered SaaS pricing captures value across SME, mid-market, and enterprise segments
Starter Plan: $499/month for SMEs with up to 10,000 customers
Professional Plan: $1,499/month for mid-market with up to 50,000 customers
Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing based on volume and requirements
Additional Revenue: API services ($0.01 per prediction), professional services
Annual contracts with 15% discount encourage longer commitments
