The churn prediction market consists of three distinct competitive tiers. Enterprise platforms like Gainsight and Salesforce dominate the high-end market but lack specialized AI capabilities. AI-powered challengers focus on predictive analytics but may lack enterprise features. The mid-market remains underserved, creating an opportunity for solutions combining AI sophistication with enterprise reliability.

Enterprise Tier: Gainsight, Salesforce, Zendesk (strong brand, weak AI)
AI-First Tier: Pecan AI, Braze, ChurnZero (strong AI, weak enterprise)
Mid-Market Gap: Underserved segment with clear opportunity
ChurnGuard AI: Superior accuracy + enterprise-grade reliability
Competitive advantages: Seamless integration, actionable insights
